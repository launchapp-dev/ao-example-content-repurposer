# Email Newsletter Snippet Template

## Platform Constraints
- Subject line: max 60 characters
- Preview text: max 90 characters (shown in inbox before opening)
- Body: 130-160 words
- CTA button text: max 5 words

## Format

```
SUBJECT: [Max 60 chars — specific and curiosity-inducing]

PREVIEW: [Max 90 chars — expands on subject, don't repeat it]

---

[BODY — 130-160 words]

[Opening sentence: direct address to reader's situation]

[2-3 short paragraphs with the key insight]

[Transition to CTA]

[CTA BUTTON TEXT: max 5 words]
[CTA URL: full article link]
```

## Tips
- Subject should not give away the full answer — create curiosity
- Preview text is prime real estate — use it to earn the open
- Body should feel like a personal note, not a press release
- CTA button text: action verbs ("Read the full story", "See how we did it")
