# LinkedIn Post Template

## Platform Constraints
- Soft limit: 1,300 characters (longer posts get truncated with "...see more")
- Hard limit: 3,000 characters
- No markdown formatting (no bold, no bullet points — use line breaks)
- Hashtags: 3-5 at the end (not mid-post)

## Format

```
[OPENING LINE — your strongest hook, no "I'm excited to share"]

[Single blank line]

[2-3 sentences expanding on the opening]

[Single blank line]

[Key insight or story — 3-4 short paragraphs with blank lines between]

[Single blank line]

[CTA or closing question to drive comments]

[Single blank line]

#hashtag1 #hashtag2 #hashtag3
```

## Tips
- First line must compel someone to click "see more"
- Use short sentences — 1-2 sentences per paragraph
- Questions in the closing drive the most engagement
- Avoid bullet points — they don't render in LinkedIn's feed
